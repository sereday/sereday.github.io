Expected Championships Added (ECA & MECA)

While most advanced statistics approximate points and wins, few account for the ultimate goal of professional basketball: championships[cite: 5]. ECA and MECA were developed to bridge this gap, recognizing that the value of a point varies significantly based on a team's baseline strength[cite: 5].

The Concept

ECA is the difference between expected team wins based on Pythagorean expectation and the expected wins after removing a player's estimated impact[cite: 5]. MECA represents the average ECA a player would attain on a typical team in a typical season[cite: 5].
